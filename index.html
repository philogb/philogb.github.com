Nico Belmonte

People and Technology leader with a 15+ year track record of building impactful products and teams in the areas of Maps, AR, VR, Data Visualization, Computer Graphics, Information Design and Machine Learning at companies like Meta, Uber and Twitter.
At Meta I lead the Geo organization (100ppl) that delivers maps to 40+M DAUs across Instagram, Facebook, WhatsApp and the next generation of Reality Labs devices. Previously at Uber, I built an organization that delivered industry-leading software for advanced visualization of business, geospatial and self-driving car data. I continued my role as an advisor to Unfolded.io, a company built on top of this software, which was acquired by Foursquare in 2021. Before this, I was a Staff Engineer at Twitter and delivered impactful open source software, technical papers and talks and mentored multiple engineers in the organization.

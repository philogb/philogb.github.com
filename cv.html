Summary

Nicolas Belmonte is Senior Engineering Manager for the Location Platform group at Facebook Reality Labs.

Nicolas' group built advanced products to explore self-driving car, geospatial and business data in the context of urban mobility. Prior to Uber Nicolas was Staff Engineer at Twitter, developing public-facing visualizations published at the NYTimes, Cannes Lions and during Twitter IPO.

Specializations: Leadership, Technical and Product Vision, Team Teaching and Building, Data Visualization Research and Development, Open Source Software, Computer Graphics.

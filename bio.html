--- layout: default title: Nicolas Garcia Belmonte ---

Nicolas Belmonte is General Manager for Maps at Mapbox. Nicolas runs product, design and engineering for the Maps organization responsible for Maps SDKs, APIs, Design and Studio. Mapbox has 500M+ monthly active users.

Prior to Mapbox Nicolas was Director of Engineering and Head of Visualization at Uber. Nicolas' group built advanced products to explore self-driving car, geospatial and business data in the context of urban mobility. Prior to Uber Nicolas was Staff Engineer at Twitter, developing public-facing visualizations published at the NYTimes, Cannes Lions and during Twitter IPO.

Nicolas' experience covers leading organizations of (50+) at Mapbox and Uber, authoring open source work, publishing research work, lecturing at universities on Visualization, speaking at conferences and exhibiting work on data art at different galleries.
